Technical Rider

Standard requirements for a smooth performance:

  • 2x Pioneer CDJ-3000 or CDJ-2000NXS2
  • 1x Pioneer DJM-900NXS2 or Allen & Heath Xone:96
  • Professional monitor speakers (booth)
  • Stable power supply with surge protection
  • Covered DJ booth (for outdoor events)
  • Minimum 2 hours soundcheck access
  • Hospitality: water, towel, secure storage

What's Included

  • Custom playlist curation for your event
  • Own backup equipment available
